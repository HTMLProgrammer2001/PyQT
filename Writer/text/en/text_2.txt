The state of Cambodia exists on the Indochina Peninsula. From ancient times the capital of this country is the city of Angkor. Angkor Wat, in Angkor, is the largest temple in the world, covering an area of ​​about 2 square kilometers! The building is decorated with five towers. The highest tower symbolizes the mythical Mount Meru, which Buddhists considered the center of the world. Today, the temple continues to receive pilgrims who are followers of Buddhist teachings.
